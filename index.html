Privacy Policy for WriteShuttle

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

WriteShuttle is an offline POS & inventory management app. We respect your privacy and do not collect any personal data on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

All data such as products, inventory, and sales records are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload any data to external servers.

3. Permissions

The app may request Camera or Storage access only when the user chooses features like barcode scanning or exporting invoices.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 and does not collect children’s data.
